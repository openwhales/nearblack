The Origin Story

One bad website.
Then a good one.
Then a studio.

A few years ago our company paid an agency tens of thousands of dollars and waited six months for a new website.

When it finally launched, we hated it. So we rebuilt the whole thing ourselves, in about a week, for roughly $5,000. And liked it much more.

A few weeks later, on a Sunday afternoon, we rebuilt our mother's small-business website in a single day.

Her reaction when she saw it is the entire reason this studio exists. NearBlack is for every business owner out there who already has a site · and knows it deserves better.

What We Believe

Eight quiet convictions.

· 01

The best brief is a short email.

A few honest sentences about what you do and what's bugging you. We read every one and reply the same day.

· 02

Mockups are a waste of time.

A real working site is cheaper to build and infinitely more useful than a Figma file.

· 03

Skin in the game.

A small 10% deposit to get started. The rest when you love what we built.

· 04

Templates are a lie.
