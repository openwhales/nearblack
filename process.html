The Process · Day 1 to Day 4

Four days. Sometimes three. Most refreshes ship in 72 hours · a handful take five days, and we tell you before we start.

Here's exactly what happens between the first email and the moment your new site goes live. No intake calls, no kickoff meetings, no discovery sessions · just a few honest emails and a working site.

Step 01 · Day Zero

A short email.

A few honest sentences sent to hello@nearblack.com · or a note through our contact form. We want to know three things: what you do, who it's for, and what's currently broken about how you present it online. That's the entire briefing. No questionnaire, no intake call.

Step 02 · Day One, Afternoon

We design.

No mood boards. No Figma files. We build a real, working, clickable website on a staging URL. By the end of the first day, you can see something. By the end of the second, you can use it.

Step 03 · Day Two or Three

You review.

Send us a voice memo. Send us a numbered list. Call us. However you want to give feedback is fine. We make the changes the same day. Most projects take one round of revisions. A few take zero.

Step 04 · Day Three or Four

We launch.

We connect your domain. We deploy to Vercel with your code on GitHub · industry standard infrastructure with automatic backups, version history, and instant rollbacks. You pay the remaining balance · and only then. If you don't love what we built, we refund your deposit. It's happened exactly zero times.

Common Questions

The fine print.

· Q

What if I don't love it?

We refund your deposit, no questions asked. We've never had a client walk away · but the guarantee is real.

· Q

Who writes the copy?

We do the first pass. You polish. On Growth and above, we include proper copy assistance.

· Q

Who hosts it?

We deploy on Vercel with your code stored on GitHub. It's the industry standard · your site auto-deploys on every update, you get version history, instant rollbacks, and automatic backups. If something ever breaks, we roll it back in seconds.

· Q

Can I edit it myself later?

Yes. We build on modern CMS platforms that a non-technical owner can comfortably update. We give you a one-page guide.

· Q

What about my existing domain?

We handle the transfer. You keep the domain. You always own everything.

· Q

Can you work faster than 72hr?

Sometimes. The Rush add-on ($500) guarantees 48-hour delivery on five-page builds, subject to availability.
